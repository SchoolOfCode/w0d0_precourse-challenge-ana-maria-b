Cardcaptors Tribute Page

A tribute to the throwback classic, Cardcaptors

Cardcaptors, originally known as ‘Cardcaptor Sakura’ started as a popular manga series in Japan in the 90’s. It tells the story of a young girl who discovers an old book containing magical cards which are released into her neighbourhood upon opening. She must use her own latent magical powers and the help of her friends to recapture the cards and save her town from catastrophe.

Having been adapted into an anime series in 1998, it was later translated for English speaking countries in 2000.

Although there were two main dubbed versions, Animax and Nelvana, it is generally agreed that Nelvana’s version is superior having been purchased and aired by powerhouses such as Cartoon Network, and Nickelodeon.

One drawback of Nelvana’s better voice acting was that they heavily censored the original series, with many episodes being taken out altogether.

Fans who wish to view all episodes are advised to watch Nelvana’s version first and then Animax’s for the ‘lost episodes’.

Cardcaptors merchandise and cosplay memorabilia is highly prized, with copies of Sakura’s magic staff being sold for over £200 - bear in mind this is a cheap piece of plastic with no real magical powers!
